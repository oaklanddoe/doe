Cybersecurity Best Practices for Autonomous Vehicle Data

LEAD: Sinclair Community College

Program Overview

This workshop introduces various cybersecurity best practices related to autonomous vehicle generated or related data. The focus of the course is centered on autonomous ground vehicles, with examples and case studies drawn from a range of mobile robotic devices.

  • Describe viable autonomous ground vehicle cybersecurity data management approaches.
  • Explore appropriate actions for protecting data from threats.
